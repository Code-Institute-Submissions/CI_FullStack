ABOUT

Have you ever heard of HYGGE ?

It is a Norwegian word pronounced ‘hoo-ga’ or ‘hue-gah’. Its meaning stretches between cozyness, wellbeing and togetherness -- but these words are not quite accurate. Hygge can be defined as creating intimacy in any given moment.

You can experience hygge watching television with a cat on your lap; sitting by the fire with a hot cup of coffee while there is a storm outside; even when having close friends over for a birthday dinner.

At CreatureComforts , we curate our different collections item with purpose, care and pleasure. We understand the power in the smell of a candle, the feel of a fabric, the sound of a chime or an heartwarming picture. When you order a box or subscribe to receive one regularly, you are inviting us to be friends and care for your wellbeing. We aim to do just that.

In the box we offer you, we wish to instill hygge via scent, light, texture and taste.

Your daily routine can be uplifted by simple actions that you do for yourself, to enjoy the moment. Feel better, be happy and welcome hygge in your life.
